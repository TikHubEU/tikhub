# tikhub
TikHub is a fast, free, and privacy-first TikTok downloader that lets you save videos in HD 1080p — completely watermark-free. No app, no sign-up, and works on any device.  Download single videos or up to 20 at once in a ZIP file, extract audio as MP3, and save slideshows or photos effortlessly. 100% free, no ads, and nothing is ever stored.
